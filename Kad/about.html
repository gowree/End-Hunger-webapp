WE ARE


Against Hunger Foundation (AHF) is a global organization .The AHF helps poor people who suffer hunger. Our main branch at Colombo in Sri Lanka. We have provided more than 30 years of service. Anyone can join us to extinction poverty and end hunger. End hunger is the second goal in sustainable development goals(SDG), so our charity accomplishes the best services to the community for success SDG2Goal: Zero Hunger.
We can’t live without food and water both are essential things for us. As well as every person needs good nutritious food to maintain good health. Unfortunately, a lot of humans are facing poverty and malnutrition. Some people are dying without food and some are ill. There are more than 690 million people who suffer hunger, as a percentage it is 8.9% and the global poverty rate is 9.2%. World hunger is rising day by day, so most people go to bed starving each night.


Our vision

Delivery fresh, nutritious and free food to hungry people and provide the best service to the community.

Green Revolution

We hope to reduce the poverty and hunger rate through the green revolution, which means we encouraged the farmers and supplying free fertilizer and seeds. After that buy their harvest at good price and distribute free that harvest to needy people.

About

Against Hunger Foundation - Our aim to end all forms of hunger and malnutrition by 2030, making sure all people–especially children–have sufficient and nutritious food all year. This involves promoting sustainable agricultural, supporting small-scale farmers and equal access to land, technology and markets.
