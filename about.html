WE ARE

Against Hunger Foundation (AHF) is a global organization .The AHF helps poor people who suffer hunger. Our main branch at Colombo in Sri Lanka. We have provided more than 30 years of service. Anyone can join us to extinction poverty and end hunger. End hunger is the second goal in sustainable Development Goals(SDG), so our charity accomplishes the best services to the community for success SDG2Goal: End Hunger.

We can’t live without food and water both are essential things for us. As well as every person needs good nutritious food to maintain good health. Unfortunately, a lot of humans are facing poverty and malnutrition. Some people are dying without food and some are ill. There are more than 690 million people who suffer hunger, as a percentage it is 8.9% and the global poverty rate is 9.2%. World hunger is rising day by day, so most people go to bed starving each night.
