WE ARE

Against Hunger Foundation (AHF) is a global organization .The AHF helps poor people who suffer hunger. Our main branch at Colombo in Sri Lanka. We have provided more than 30 years of service. Anyone can join us to extinction poverty and end hunger. End hunger is the second goal in sustainable Development Goals(SDG), so our survice accomplishes the best services to the community for success SDG2Goal: End Hunger.

The number of people suffering from malnutritionhas droped by almost half in the last two decades due to rapid economic growth and rising productivity of agricultural produce. Many developing countries suffering from famine and stravation can now meet their nutritional needs. Sustainable Development Goal aims to end all forms of hunger and malnutrioun by 2030, ensuring that all people- especially children-have adequate and nutritious food throught the year.

We can’t live without food and water both are essential things for us. As well as every person needs good nutritious food to maintain good health. Unfortunately, a lot of humans are facing poverty and malnutrition. Some people are dying without food and some are ill. There are more than 690 million people who suffer hunger, as a percentage it is 8.9% and the global poverty rate is 9.2%. World hunger is rising day by day, so most people go to bed starving each night.

Green Revolution

We hope to reduce the poverty and hunger rate through the green revolution, which means we encouraged the farmers and supplying free fertilizer and seeds. After that buy their harvest at good price and distribute free that harvest to needy people.
